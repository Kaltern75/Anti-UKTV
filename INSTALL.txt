AntiUKTV - Installation
=======================

This hides BBC, ITV and Channel 4 channels from YouTube.

1. You have already unzipped this folder (AntiUKTV). Keep it somewhere
   permanent - if you delete or move it later, the extension stops working.

2. Open your browser and go to one of these addresses:
      Chrome:  chrome://extensions
      Edge:    edge://extensions

3. Turn ON "Developer mode" (a switch in the top-right corner).

4. Click "Load unpacked".

5. Select this AntiUKTV folder and click Select Folder.

Done. Open YouTube - BBC / ITV / Channel 4 content will be gone.
Click the AntiUKTV toolbar icon any time to switch it on or off.

Note: because this is a "developer mode" extension, your browser may show a
reminder about it now and then - that is normal and safe to dismiss.
